Kadhi-Chawal

Description

Kadhi Chawal is a popular and comforting Indian dish that combines a tangy yogurt-based curry, known as kadhi, with steamed rice (chawal). The kadhi is made by whisking yogurt with gram flour (besan) and spices, then simmering it until thickened and flavorful. It's often garnished with fresh coriander and served hot alongside fluffy rice. This dish is not only delicious but also nutritious, making it a staple in many Indian households. Perfect for a wholesome meal, Kadhi Chawal is loved for its unique blend of flavors and creamy texture.

Kadhi Ingredients

  • Yogurt (curd) - 2 cups
  • Gram flour (besan) - ½ cup
  • Water - 4 cups (adjust for desired consistency)
  • Turmeric powder - ½ teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Fenugreek seeds (optional) - ½ teaspoon
  • Curry leaves - a few (optional)
  • Ginger - 1 inch, grated
  • Green chilies - 1-2, slit
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil or ghee - for tempering
  • Fresh coriander - for garnish
    • Rice -as needed

    Steps

    1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Whisk until smooth.
    2. Add water to the mixture to get a pouring consistency.
    3. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
    4. Add ginger-garlic paste and curry leaves, sauté for another minute.
    5. Pour in the yogurt-besan mixture and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
    6. Cook on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    7. Once it thickens, turn off the heat and set aside.
    8. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
    9. In a pot, add the rinsed rice, water, and salt.
    10. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the water is absorbed.
    11. Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.
