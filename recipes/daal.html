Daal

Description

Daal is a flavorful and nutritious dish made from lentils, often seasoned with spices and herbs. It is a staple in South Asian cuisine, known for its rich protein content and comforting texture, typically served with rice or flatbreads

Ingredients

  • Lentils (red, green, or yellow)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Rinse 1 cup lentils under cold water until clear.
  2. In a pot, add lentils and 3 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  3. In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons oil or ghee.
  4. Add 1 teaspoon cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  5. Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  6. Stir in 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste and 2 slit green chilies. Cook for 1 minute.
  7. Add 2 chopped tomatoes, 1 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, and salt to taste. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  8. Pour the sautéed mixture into the pot with lentils.
  9. Stir well and simmer for 15-20 minutes, adjusting water for desired consistency.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or roti.
