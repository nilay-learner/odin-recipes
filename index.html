Kadhi-Chawal daal

Description

Daal is a flavorful and nutritious dish made from lentils, often seasoned with spices and herbs. It is a staple in South Asian cuisine, known for its rich protein content and comforting texture, typically served with rice or flatbreads.

Ingredients

  • Lentils (red, green, or yellow)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies; cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt; cook until tomatoes soften.
  6. Add the rinsed lentils and enough water to cover them. Bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat and simmer until the lentils are tender, about 20-30 minutes.
  8. Stir in garam masala and adjust seasoning to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
